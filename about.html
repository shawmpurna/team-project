Our Story

Our Mission

At Street Greek, we believe great food should be accessible to everyone. We're passionate about serving authentic Greek street food that's fresh, flavorful, and made with love. Every dish tells a story of tradition and innovation.

Fresh & Local

We source the finest ingredients from local suppliers whenever possible. Our pitas are baked fresh daily, our meats are carefully selected, and our vegetables are always crisp and seasonal. Quality is never compromised.

The Team

Our kitchen is led by Chef Maria, who brings 15 years of experience from her family's restaurant in Athens. She's joined by a passionate team dedicated to creating the perfect Greek dining experience for every customer.
